Your privacy matters

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026  ·  Effective: April 12, 2026

🔒 The short version: We collect only what we need to run Kibai, we never sell your data to anyone, and you can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing kibaisupport@gmail.com.

1. Who We Are

Kibai ("we", "us", "our") is an AI-powered dog nutrition application developed and operated by LLK Ventures. When you use Kibai — whether through our website (kibai.app) or any associated services — this Privacy Policy governs how we handle your personal information.

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at kibaisupport@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist, create an account, or make a purchase.
  • Name — when you fill out contact forms or create a profile.
  • Dog photos — uploaded by you for AI analysis.
  • Dog profile data — breed, age, weight, activity level, and any health notes you enter.
  • Messages — when you contact us via our contact form or email.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information — browser type, operating system, device model.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, session duration, click events.
  • IP address — used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate geolocation (country/region only).
  • Cookies — see Section 7 for details.

2.3 Payment Information

Purchases are processed by Gumroad, a third-party payment processor. We never see or store your credit card number, CVV, or full payment details. Gumroad's privacy policy governs their handling of your payment data.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Kibai AI nutrition analysis service.
  • Analyze your dog's photo to generate breed identification, BCS scoring, and nutrition recommendations.
  • Send you transactional emails (order confirmations, access credentials).
  • Send product updates and newsletters — only if you have opted in.
  • Respond to your support inquiries and contact form submissions.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access.
  • Improve our AI models — but only with your explicit consent (we will always ask separately).
  • Comply with legal obligations.

4. Dog Photos & AI Processing

Photos you upload are transmitted securely over TLS to our AI processing servers for analysis. Once analysis is complete:

  • Photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours of processing.
  • Only the analysis results (breed, BCS score, calorie recommendations) are stored against your account.
  • We do not use your dog's photos to train our AI models without explicit, separately obtained consent. You will always be asked first.
  • Photos are never shared with third parties for any purpose.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database provider. Data is stored in encrypted, secure environments. Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Gumroad — for payment processing. Gumroad Privacy Policy.
  • Plausible Analytics — privacy-first analytics that does not use cookies or fingerprinting. No personal data is collected by Plausible.
  • Legal authorities — only when required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users or the public.

6. Data Security

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • All data stored in our database is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Our Supabase database has Row Level Security (RLS) enabled, enforcing strict access controls.
  • We conduct regular security reviews and keep all dependencies updated.
  • Access to user data by our team is limited to the minimum necessary and is logged.

Despite our best efforts, no system is 100% secure. If you believe your data has been compromised, contact us immediately at kibaisupport@gmail.com.

7. Cookies

We use only essential and analytics cookies:

  • Session cookies — necessary for you to stay logged in. These expire when you close your browser.
  • Plausible Analytics — tracks page views and events without storing personal data, using no cookies at all.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, Facebook Pixel, Google Ads tags, or any third-party marketing cookies.

8. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt-out — unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email.
  • Withdraw consent — if you previously consented to AI training use of your photos, withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email kibaisupport@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days (and within 72 hours for urgent requests).

GDPR (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority.

CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, to delete it, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

9. Data Retention

  • Dog photos: deleted within 24 hours of analysis.
  • Account data: retained for as long as your account is active, or until you request deletion.
  • Waitlist emails: retained until the waitlist closes or you unsubscribe.
  • Support messages: retained for up to 2 years for quality and legal purposes.
  • Analytics data: aggregated, non-identifiable data retained indefinitely.

10. Children's Privacy

Kibai is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we learn we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with their data, contact kibaisupport@gmail.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by email (to the address on file) and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
