Our Commitment to You

We want every dog parent to feel 100% confident in their purchase. We built Kibai because we love dogs — and if it doesn't help yours, we'll make it right. No hoops, no drama.

🛡️ 30-Day Full Money-Back Guarantee

1. Our Guarantee

All Kibai plans — Basic ($5), Popular ($9), and Founder/Lifetime ($20) — come with a 30-day full money-back guarantee. No questions asked. No justification required.

If you are not satisfied with Kibai for any reason within 30 days of your purchase, you are entitled to a complete refund of the amount you paid.

2. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple and fast:

  1. Email us at kibaisupport@gmail.com from the email address you used to purchase.
  2. Include your Gumroad order number (found in your purchase confirmation email — it looks like "GUM-XXXXXX").
  3. Optionally, tell us what went wrong — we genuinely read every message and use your feedback to improve Kibai.

That's it. We will process your refund within 2–3 business days, and it will appear back in your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank.

💡 Alternative: You can also use our Contact Form to request a refund. Select "Refund Request" as the subject.

3. Refund Timeline

  • Eligibility window: 30 days from the date of purchase (not the date of first use).
  • Processing time: 2–3 business days from when we receive your request.
  • Return to card: 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.
  • Access: Your account access will be revoked upon refund confirmation.

4. After the 30-Day Window

Refund requests submitted after 30 days of purchase are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We will consider refunds or partial credits if:

  • You experienced a significant technical issue that prevented you from using the Service and you notified us within 30 days of that issue occurring.
  • You purchased in error (e.g., accidental double purchase).
  • There were extraordinary circumstances that prevented you from using the Service through no fault of your own.

We're humans — we'll always try to find a fair resolution. Email us and we'll discuss it.

5. Lifetime Plan — Special Commitment

For our Founder (Lifetime) plan holders, we make an additional commitment:

  • If Kibai permanently discontinues the Service within 2 years of your Lifetime purchase, you will automatically receive a pro-rated refund based on the time remaining — no request needed from you.
  • This refund will be calculated as: ($20 × remaining months / 24 months) and issued to your original payment method.
  • We will give at least 90 days' advance notice before any permanent shutdown.

We stand fully behind our lifetime promise. This commitment is binding on LLK Ventures.

6. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will generally not be issued in the following cases (though we always review with good faith):

  • You have violated our Terms & Conditions, resulting in account termination.
  • The refund request is clearly fraudulent or abusive (e.g., using the service fully for 29 days then requesting a refund repeatedly across multiple accounts).
  • Requests made more than 60 days after purchase without documented extenuating circumstances.

7. Chargebacks

We strongly encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. We will resolve legitimate issues faster than the chargeback process and without any adverse marks on your account.

Unauthorized chargebacks (where a refund could have been obtained through our standard process) may result in:

  • Immediate revocation of account access.
  • Being prohibited from future purchases.

We're always reachable at kibaisupport@gmail.com — please reach out first.

8. Currency & Payment Method

All refunds are issued in USD to the original payment method used at purchase. If currency conversion rates have changed since your purchase, refund amounts may differ slightly in your local currency — this is outside our control.

Payments are processed by Gumroad. Refunds may appear on your statement as "Gumroad" or "Gumroad*Kibai".

9. Contact for Refunds

  • 📧 kibaisupport@gmail.com (dedicated refunds inbox)
  • 📧 kibaisupport@gmail.com (general inquiries)
  • 🌐 Contact Form

We respond to all refund requests within 1 business day.
